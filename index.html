QR Code-Enabled BIM-Based Digital Construction Tracking System:
This data sheet is part of a QR code-enabled digital construction tracking system. A QR code is physically attached to this structural element on site and, when scanned, opens this live record directly on any mobile device. The system links the physical element to its digital twin in the federated Revit BIM model, providing real-time access to construction status, QA test results, material delivery records, site issues and phased approval sign-offs. Any change made in the admin system is reflected here automatically. This replaces paper site records and ensures all team members are working from the same verified, up-to-date information, in full compliance with EN ISO 19650 information management requirements.
